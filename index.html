About Me: Steven Boston

Competitive Magic: the Gathering

I have been a competitive magic player for many years, and have won tournaments of varying levels across the state of Washington, and played in events across the country. I have also qualified for and participated in two pro tours.

Judge and Event Planner

When I am not battling myself, I also enjoy doing event plannig work, and serve many organizations as both an event organizer and judge. I also fill roles as a sales representative and teacher for board and card game companies at gaming conventions.
